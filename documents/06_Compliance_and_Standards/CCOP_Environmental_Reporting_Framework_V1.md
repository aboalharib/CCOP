# 1 Purpose

This document defines the environmental reporting framework supported by the Climate Change Operation Platform (CCOP).

The purpose of this framework is to describe how environmental monitoring data collected by CCOP can support national and international environmental reporting requirements.

---

# 2 Scope

This reporting framework applies to environmental data generated by:

- TerraNode monitoring stations
- TerraEdge monitoring stations
- TerraAir portable monitoring devices
- reference monitoring stations integrated into the CCOP network

The framework supports environmental reporting, climate monitoring, and air-quality management programs.

---

# 3 Environmental Monitoring Data Sources

Environmental datasets produced by the CCOP monitoring network include:

| Data Category | Example Parameters |
|---|---|
Air Quality | PM1, PM2.5, PM10, O₃, NO₂, SO₂, CO, NH₃, HF, H₂S |
Meteorological | temperature, humidity, pressure, wind speed |
Environmental | UV index, noise level |
System Metadata | device status, sensor lifecycle information |

These datasets support environmental monitoring and reporting.

# 6 International Environmental Reporting Context

Many countries are required to produce environmental reports under international climate and environmental governance frameworks.

Key reporting frameworks include:

| Framework | Description |
|---|---|
National Greenhouse Gas Inventory | national emissions reporting |
Biennial Transparency Report (BTR) | climate reporting under UN frameworks |
Nationally Determined Contributions (NDC) | climate mitigation commitments |
National Adaptation Plan (NAP) | climate adaptation strategies |
Air Quality Monitoring Reports | environmental air-quality reporting |

Environmental monitoring systems such as CCOP provide the underlying datasets used to generate these reports.

---

# 7 Environmental Data Quality Requirements

Environmental reporting requires reliable data.

Data quality measures include:

- calibration and correction models
- reference station comparison
- data validation procedures
- lifecycle monitoring of sensors

These mechanisms ensure reporting reliability.

---

# 8 CCOP Climate Intelligence Platform Role

The CCOP Climate Intelligence Platform (CIP) processes monitoring data and produces reporting-ready datasets.

Capabilities include:

- environmental data aggregation
- statistical analysis
- environmental trend analysis
- report generation

These capabilities allow environmental agencies to generate reports efficiently.
