# 5 Monitoring Stations

## TerraNode

TerraNode stations provide the most complete environmental observations.

Operational roles:

- air-quality monitoring
- meteorological monitoring
- reference environmental measurements

TerraNode acts as the **reference monitoring station** in the monitoring network.

---

## TerraEdge

TerraEdge stations extend monitoring coverage across distributed locations.

Operational roles:

- distributed air quality monitoring
- environmental observation

---

## TerraAir

TerraAir stations support portable and temporary monitoring.

Operational roles:

- mobile monitoring
- temporary environmental observation

---

# 6 Environmental Monitoring Pyramid

The monitoring network follows a three-tier monitoring model.

| Tier | Station | Role |
|------|------|------|
| Tier 1 | TerraNode | reference monitoring station |
| Tier 2 | TerraEdge | distributed monitoring station |
| Tier 3 | TerraAir | portable monitoring station |


Tier 1 → TerraNode → Reference Monitoring Station
Tier 2 → TerraEdge → Distributed Monitoring Station
Tier 3 → TerraAir → Portable Monitoring Station


All stations feed telemetry to the centralized platform.

---

# 7 Monitoring Workflow

Monitoring stations collect environmental observations and transmit telemetry to the centralized platform.

Operational workflow:


Monitoring Stations
↓
Telemetry Transmission
↓
Streaming Infrastructure
↓

Dashboards / Reporting / Analytics

# 8 Sensor Calibration Operations

Calibration activities occur throughout the monitoring station lifecycle.

Calibration processes include:

- factory calibration verification
- TerraNode reference comparison
- algorithmic calibration adjustment

Calibration ensures measurement consistency across the monitoring network.

---

# 9 Environmental Data Governance Operations

Environmental data governance ensures telemetry remains reliable and traceable.

Governance activities include:

- telemetry validation
- calibration traceability
- dataset version tracking
- reporting dataset validation

These processes maintain environmental reporting credibility.

---

# 10 Environmental Reporting Operations

Telemetry collected by monitoring stations is aggregated to generate reporting datasets.

Reporting outputs may include:

- air quality monitoring reports
- climate monitoring datasets
- greenhouse gas monitoring datasets

# 12 System Simulation Operations

Before pilot deployment, the CCOP system shall support a full simulation phase.

Simulation operations include:

- simulated TerraNode reference stations
- simulated TerraEdge distributed stations
- simulated TerraAir portable stations
- synthetic telemetry generation
- anomaly and alert simulation
- calibration drift simulation
- reporting workflow simulation

The simulation environment validates system behavior before field deployment.
