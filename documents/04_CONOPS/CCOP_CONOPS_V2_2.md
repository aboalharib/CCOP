# 8 Monitoring Stations

## 8.1 CCOP TerraNode

A fixed monitoring station equipped with meteorological sensors and environmental monitoring sensors.

Operational role:

- continuous monitoring
- air-quality observation
- meteorological monitoring
- reference environmental observations

TerraNode acts as the **reference monitoring station** within the monitoring network.

---

## 8.2 CCOP TerraEdge

A fixed environmental monitoring station similar to TerraNode but without meteorological sensors.

Operational role:

- distributed monitoring coverage
- air-quality monitoring

---

## 8.3 CCOP TerraAir

A portable environmental monitoring station designed for temporary or mobile deployments.

Operational role:

- temporary monitoring
- mobile environmental observation
- field monitoring missions

---

# 9 CCOP Environmental Monitoring Pyramid

The CCOP monitoring network follows a **three-tier monitoring model** used in modern smart city environmental monitoring systems.

| Tier | Station | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Tier 1 | TerraNode | Reference monitoring station |
| Tier 2 | TerraEdge | Distributed monitoring station |
| Tier 3 | TerraAir | Portable monitoring station |

TerraNode stations provide the most complete environmental observations and act as **reference monitoring stations**.

TerraEdge stations extend monitoring coverage across fixed deployment locations.

TerraAir stations support mobile and temporary monitoring deployments.

Tier 1 → TerraNode → Reference Monitoring Station
Tier 2 → TerraEdge → Distributed Monitoring Station
Tier 3 → TerraAir → Portable Monitoring Station

# 10 Operational Monitoring Model

The CCOP monitoring system operates through distributed monitoring stations transmitting telemetry to a centralized platform.

Monitoring stations collect environmental measurements including air-quality indicators and meteorological parameters.

Telemetry is transmitted through available communication infrastructure to the CCOP Climate Intelligence Platform.

# 13 Coastal and Offshore Monitoring Scenario

In this scenario, monitoring stations are deployed in coastal and offshore environments to observe environmental conditions affecting maritime infrastructure and marine ecosystems.

Examples include:

- TerraNode stations installed on offshore industrial platforms
- TerraNode stations installed along coastal monitoring sites
- monitoring stations deployed near ports and shipping corridors
