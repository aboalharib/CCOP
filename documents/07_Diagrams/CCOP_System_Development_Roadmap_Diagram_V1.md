The roadmap illustrates how the CCOP system evolves from design and documentation through prototype development, pilot deployment, and full operational monitoring networks.

# 3 Device Development Timeline

| Device | Development Stage |
|---|---|
TerraNode | prototype → regional monitoring station |
TerraEdge | prototype → distributed monitoring device |
TerraAir | prototype → portable monitoring device |

All devices integrate into the CCOP monitoring architecture.

---

# 4 Backend Platform Evolution

The CCOP Climate Intelligence Platform evolves through several stages.

| Stage | Capability |
|---|---|
Prototype | telemetry ingestion and storage |
Pilot | dashboards and monitoring |
Operational | analytics and reporting |
Expansion | large-scale environmental data processing |

---

# 5 Monitoring Network Growth

The monitoring network grows progressively.

| Stage | Deployment |
|---|---|
Prototype | laboratory testing |
Pilot | limited monitoring network |
Operational | regional monitoring coverage |
Expansion | large-scale environmental monitoring |

---

# 6 Roadmap Benefits

The CCOP development roadmap provides:

- structured system evolution
- controlled technology deployment
- validation before large-scale expansion
- predictable development milestones

This phased approach reduces technical risk.
